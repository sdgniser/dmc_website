Drama 'N' Music Club

[WORK IN PROGRESS...] The Drama and Music Club of NISER, Bhubaneswar, was formed for the purpose of bringing together people, who share a common appreciation for art, and help each other, in developing these co-curricular skills. Our Club introduces students into the magical world of theatre and music, where they can explore a range of fun activities, while honing their artistic knowledge amidst the monotonous academic schedule.
