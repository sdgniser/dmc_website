SPIC MACAY (NISER)

SPIC MACAY (Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music and Culture Amongst Youth) is a non-political organisation, aimed at bringing Indian Classical culture to the youth of today. NISER is a proud part of this endeavour and works closely on organising events, through SPICMACAY, to bring to attention the fading Indian Culture, to the students here. For further information on SPICMACAY, please click on the link in the heading, or reach out to us, through our social links!
